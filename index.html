What is the probability that I get in my dream graduate school given my status and my GPA?


In the picture above, the parameters of the gaussian are used to model how hard it is to get in the school (a high
GPA and a small deviance for example, indicates a hard college to get into). I use this gaussian model paired with
the probability of an international student getting in the particular school to design a model to predict the chance
of a random student to get in a top school just given the these two variables. The values of parameters mu and sigma are
fixed given the university considered by the student.

I used the data provided by gradcafe to make the model and the prediction.
